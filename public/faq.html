Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ClawSwap for AI agents.

Getting Started

How do I integrate ClawSwap into my AI agent?

Send this to your agent: Read https://clawswap.tech/skill.md and follow the instructions to enable ClawSwap.

Your agent will learn how to swap and bridge tokens across all supported chains. Integration takes about 15 minutes.

Does my agent need a wallet?

Yes. ClawSwap is designed for AI agents with wallet capabilities (Solana and EVM wallets). If your agent doesn't have a wallet, you'll need to set one up first.

Do I need an API key?

No. ClawSwap is completely permissionless. No signup, no API key, no KYC.

Swaps & Bridges

What blockchains are supported?

ClawSwap supports 9+ major blockchains:

  • Solana
  • Ethereum
  • Base
  • Arbitrum
  • Optimism
  • Polygon
  • BSC (Binance Smart Chain)
  • Avalanche
  • Sui

How long do swaps take?

Same-chain swaps: Near-instant (2-10 seconds)
Cross-chain swaps: Under 10 seconds

Most cross-chain swaps complete in under 10 seconds.

What's the fee?

  • Solana swaps: 1% (100 bps)
  • EVM swaps: 0.5% (50 bps)

Fees are included in the quote. No hidden fees, no gas surprises.

Why am I getting wrapped tokens (WSOL, WETH)?

Use the zero address for native tokens:

"fromToken": "0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000"

This ensures you receive SOL, ETH, BNB, etc. instead of wrapped versions.

Security & Trust

Is ClawSwap custodial?

No. ClawSwap is completely non-custodial. Your tokens move directly from your wallet through on-chain smart contracts to the destination. ClawSwap never holds your funds.

Who built ClawSwap?

ClawSwap is built on Mayan Finance - battle-tested cross-chain infrastructure secured by Wormhole guardians and Circle CCTP. Millions in daily volume.

Can I verify my swaps on-chain?

Yes. Every swap is a blockchain transaction with a transaction hash (txHash). You can verify it on any block explorer.

What if a swap fails?

If a swap fails, your tokens are automatically refunded to your wallet. No funds are lost.

Integration & Technical

Can I use ClawSwap with any AI model?

Yes. ClawSwap works with ChatGPT, Claude, custom agents, Telegram bots, Discord bots, and any LLM that can make HTTP requests.

Are there rate limits?

No. ClawSwap is designed for high-frequency agent trading. Swap as often as you need.

Yes! Check out ClawSend - our companion product for launching tokens on Solana in 1 transaction.

Can I test ClawSwap without real funds?

Yes. Start with a small amount (0.01 SOL) to test. All swaps are on mainnet, so use real but small amounts to verify functionality.

Troubleshooting

My swap is taking longer than 10 seconds. What's wrong?

Cross-chain swaps can occasionally take 2-3 minutes during network congestion. Check the swap status using GET /v1/swap/:id.

I got an error: "Invalid chain". What does this mean?

Make sure you're using lowercase chain names: solana, ethereum, base, arbitrum, optimism, polygon, bsc, avalanche, sui.

How do I swap native tokens (SOL, ETH, BNB)?

Use the zero address:

"fromToken": "0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000"

This works for all native tokens on all chains.

Can I cancel a swap?

No. Once a swap is submitted to the blockchain, it cannot be cancelled. Make sure your quote looks correct before executing.
